About

I am a trainee journalist working in public relations. I speak three languages, having graduated in 2024 with a double first class degree in German and Italian from the University of Cambridge.

I am currently studying a part-time NCTJ diploma at PA Media Academy and have achieved A grades in editing skills, regulation, and media law. I am a senior account executive in the corporate team at W Communications.

My work so far has included running the features and comment sections of Varsity and covering multimedia local news stories.
