About me

I am a trainee journalist working in public relations. I graduated in 2024 with a double first class degree in German and Italian from the University of Cambridge.

I am currently studying a part-time NCTJ diploma at PA Media Academy and have achieved A grades in editing skills, regulation and media law. I am a senior account executive in the corporate team at W Communications.

My work spans reported news, features and comment, with particular interests in culture, environment, politics and social issues.
